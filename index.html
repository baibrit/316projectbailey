Riley County has vaccination rate of 583, 34th highest rate in Kansas

By Bailey Britton, Hallie Everett, Lexi Garcia

November 17, 2021 | Source: KDHE

As of November 10, Riley County has the 34th highest vaccination rate in Kansas at 586 vaccinated per 1,000 people. This rate is calculated for those age 12 and older with at least one dose of the vaccine.

For the student perspective, we will be interviewing a student advocating for the vaccine and a student who is against vaccine mandates.
